E-Commerce Inventory Management System

The idea behind this project is to allow E-Commerce Retailers to keep track of the amount of stock present for each product they sell on multiple E-Commerce platforms such as Flipkart, Amazon and so on. Upon keeping track of the stock present, dead stock (stock which isn't being sold and is present in the inventory for a long time) can be removed and in situtations of natural disasters, stocks can be identified and kept out seperately to help the needy.
